Safety Center

Safety First

At Mortalive, your safety and well-being are our top priority. We work hard to keep the platform respectful and secure.

Community Guidelines

Be Kind and Respectful
Treat others the way you want to be treated. No hate speech, harassment, or bullying.

18+ Only
Mortalive is strictly for adults. Anyone under 18 is not allowed.

No Personal Information
Do not share phone numbers, addresses, or any private details.

Privacy Policy

Anonymous by Default
You can use Mortalive without creating an account. Guests remain completely anonymous.

What We Collect
We only collect the minimum data needed to run the service. Chat messages are not stored permanently. Snapshots are used only for moderation and deleted after review.

Your Data is Protected
We do not sell your data to third parties.

Safety Features

Instant Skip
You can leave any chat at any time with one click.

Report Button
Report inappropriate behavior during a chat. Our team reviews reports quickly.

Snapshot Monitoring
Random screenshots are taken during video chats to help moderation (only for safety, not stored permanently).

AI + Human Moderation
We combine automated tools and human review to keep the platform safe.

Camera & Microphone

Permissions
When you start video chat, your browser will ask for camera and microphone access. You must grant permission to use video mode.

Local Mirror
Your own video is mirrored so you can see yourself as others see you (you can flip it if needed).

Camera Off Option
You can turn off your camera or microphone at any time during a chat.

Tips for Staying Safe

• Never share personal information
• Trust your instincts — leave if something feels wrong
• Use the report button for bad behavior
• Keep conversations light and respectful
• Take breaks if needed
• Remember that everyone is anonymous — don’t take things too personally
Your safety matters. Report any issues immediately. Return to Mortalive
