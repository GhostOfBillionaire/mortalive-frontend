Your Safety First

Our Safety Promise

Mortalive is built with privacy and safety at its core. We actively work to prevent harassment, scams, and harmful behavior.

🔒 Private by Design

Your identity stays protected until you choose to share more. We never force video or location sharing.

🛡️ Active Moderation

AI + human moderation team monitor for violations 24/7.

🚨 Easy Reporting

Report any user or conversation with one tap. We respond quickly.

Safety Features

1. Consent-First Matching

Both users must agree before entering a video call or sharing personal details.

2. Block & Report

Instantly block anyone. All reports are reviewed within hours.

3. Photo & Profile Verification

Optional verification helps build trust within the community.

4. Safe Exit

One-tap exit button available during every video call.

Community Guidelines

We do not tolerate:

  • Harassment, bullying, or hate speech
  • Scams or solicitation
  • Explicit content without consent
  • Impersonation or fake profiles
  • Any form of illegal activity
