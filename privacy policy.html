Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data. For guests, we collect no personal information. For registered users, we store username, hashed password, and Magnet Score.

2. How We Use Your Data

Data is used only to provide the service, improve matching, and maintain safety through moderation.

3. Snapshots & Moderation

During video chats, random snapshots may be taken for moderation purposes. These are deleted after review and are not stored long-term.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Information is only shared with trusted service providers when necessary for platform operation.

5. Your Rights

You can delete your account at any time. Guest users have no persistent data stored.

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use of the service means you accept the updated policy.
