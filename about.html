Our Story

About Mortalive

Mortalive is a modern anonymous random chat platform for video and text conversations. We rebuilt the classic stranger chat experience with better design, stronger safety, and a focus on genuine human connection.

Back to app Start Chatting

Our Mission

We believe anonymous chat can be both fun and respectful.

Mortalive was created to bring back the joy of talking to strangers without the toxicity and chaos of older platforms. Our goal is to create a safe space where people can have real conversations, share ideas, practice languages, or simply pass the time with someone new. We prioritize user safety, respectful behavior, and a clean, modern experience.

Why Mortalive?

A better alternative to older random chat sites.

Unlike outdated platforms, Mortalive offers a fresh interface, fast matching, mobile-first design, and strong moderation tools. You can chat as a guest instantly or create an account to build a Magnet Score based on positive interactions. Video and text modes are both available with easy controls.

How It Works

Simple flow from landing to chat.

1. Accept the consent prompt on the landing page.
2. Choose Text or Video mode in the lobby.
3. Optionally add interests for better matching.
4. Get paired with a stranger instantly.
5. Chat freely and rate the conversation when finished.
6. Leave or find the next person anytime.

Safety First

We take moderation seriously.

We use snapshot monitoring during video chats, user reports, and quick action on violations. All chats are anonymous by default. We have a strict 18+ policy and work hard to keep the platform respectful and safe for everyone.

The Technology

Modern stack for a smooth experience

Mortalive is built with modern, reliable web technologies to deliver a fast and smooth experience. We use WebRTC for high-quality video calls, Socket.io for real-time messaging, and a clean single-file frontend architecture that loads quickly on both desktop and mobile devices. The backend is powered by a lightweight Node.js server with Supabase handling data and real-time features. Everything is optimized for performance, security, and ease of use.

Our Vision

The future we are building.

We want Mortalive to become the most trusted anonymous chat platform in the world. In the future we plan to add more features like voice mode, better matching algorithms, and community-driven improvements. Our long-term goal is to create a global space for meaningful human connections.
