Our Story • 2026

About Mortalive

Mortalive is a modern anonymous random chat platform designed for genuine human connections. We rebuilt the classic stranger chat experience with better design, stronger safety tools, and a focus on respect and privacy.

Our Mission

At Mortalive, we believe that talking to strangers should be simple, safe, and enjoyable. In a world full of social media noise and superficial interactions, we created a space where people can have real, unfiltered conversations without pressure or judgment. Our mission is to bring back the magic of random chat while making it safer and more respectful for everyone.

Why Mortalive Exists

Older platforms like Omegle became unsafe and outdated. We saw the need for a new generation of anonymous chat — one that prioritizes user safety, modern design, fast performance on mobile, and meaningful connections. Mortalive was built from the ground up in 2026 to fill that gap.

How Mortalive Works

The experience is intentionally simple:

1. Visit the site and accept our quick consent prompt.
2. Choose between Text Chat or Video Chat.
3. Optionally add interests to find better matches.
4. Get paired with a stranger instantly.
5. Chat freely, rate the conversation when you're done, and move on to the next person.

No complicated accounts required to start. Guests can jump in immediately.

Safety & Moderation

Safety is at the core of everything we do. We use a combination of automated tools (including periodic snapshots during video chats for moderation review), user reports, and human oversight. All chats are anonymous by default. We have a strict 18+ policy and take swift action against harassment, inappropriate content, or abuse. Users can leave any conversation at any time with one click.

The Technology Stack

Mortalive is built with modern, reliable technologies:

• WebRTC for high-quality video calls
• Socket.io for real-time messaging
• Supabase for backend services and database
• A lightweight, single-file frontend for fast loading

Everything is optimized for both desktop and mobile devices, with special attention to performance on phones.

Our Vision for the Future

We want Mortalive to become the most trusted anonymous chat platform in the world. In the coming months we plan to add features like interest-based smart matching, voice-only mode, improved moderation dashboard, community guidelines shaped by users, and more ways to connect meaningfully. Long-term, our goal is to create a global space where millions of people can discover new friends, ideas, and perspectives every single day — safely and respectfully.

The Team Behind Mortalive

Mortalive was created by a small, passionate team of developers, designers, and product thinkers who believe in the power of human connection. We keep our team relatively anonymous for now to focus on the product rather than personalities, but we are fully committed to transparency with our users, listening to feedback, and continuously improving the platform based on real needs.
