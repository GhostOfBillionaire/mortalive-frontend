Welcome to the Mortalive Blog

Made in India • Building the future of anonymous social connection

We are a passionate Indian startup on a mission to create one of the safest and most enjoyable anonymous social platforms in the world. Mortalive combines the thrill of random chat with strong safety measures, live streaming features, and opportunities for creators to earn money through content, followers, and meaningful interactions.

Why We Built Mortalive

A safer, modern social app startup from India

In today's digital world, people are looking for authentic connections without the pressure of permanent profiles. As a Startup India initiative, we created Mortalive to offer a safer alternative to traditional anonymous chat apps. Our platform allows users to enjoy text chat, video chat, and live streaming while maintaining full anonymity and strong community guidelines. We believe technology should bring people closer in positive and respectful ways.

Live Streaming & Creator Economy

Earn money with content on Mortalive

One of the most exciting features of Mortalive is live streaming. Creators can go live, interact with their followers in real time, and build a loyal audience. Through our messaging system and follower tools, users can earn money by creating engaging content, hosting interactive sessions, and growing their community. This creator-first approach makes Mortalive more than just a chat app — it is a complete social experience.

Safety at the Core

Our commitment to a safer social platform

We understand that safety is the foundation of any successful anonymous social app. That's why we have implemented strong moderation, snapshot monitoring during video sessions, quick report handling, and clear community rules. As an Indian startup, we are especially focused on creating a platform that respects Indian values while providing a global stage for users to connect safely and freely.

Our Journey as a Startup

From idea to a growing social platform

Starting as a small team with a big dream, Mortalive is proud to be part of the Startup India movement. We are building a platform that combines the best of anonymous chat, live streaming, and creator tools. Our vision is to grow into one of India's most loved social apps, giving millions of users a safe place to chat, stream, and earn through their content and community.
