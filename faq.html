Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using Mortalive

What is Mortalive? +
Mortalive is a calm, privacy-first social platform designed for meaningful video conversations and genuine connections. We focus on quality interactions rather than endless scrolling.
Is Mortalive free to use? +
Yes, the core experience is completely free. We may introduce optional premium features in the future, but basic matching and conversations will always remain free.
How does privacy work on Mortalive? +
Your identity is protected by default. You control what you share. We do not store video or audio recordings, and camera/microphone access is only granted when you start a call.
Do I need to turn on my camera? +
No. You can choose to keep your camera off. Many users start with audio-only conversations and enable video only when they feel comfortable.
How do you keep the community safe? +
We combine AI moderation with human review. Users can report others instantly, and we take swift action against harassment, scams, or inappropriate behavior.
Can I block or report someone? +
Yes. You can block any user with one tap, and reporting is available during and after every conversation. All reports are reviewed seriously.
What age do I need to be to use Mortalive? +
You must be at least 18 years old to create an account and use the platform.
Do you store my conversations? +
No. We do not record or store video or audio from your calls. Text messages are only stored temporarily for delivery and then deleted.
