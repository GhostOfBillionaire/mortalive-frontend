Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about how Mortalive works, safety, accounts, and more.

General Questions

What is Mortalive?
Mortalive is a modern anonymous chat platform where you can talk to strangers through text or video. It is designed to be safer, cleaner, and more enjoyable than older random chat sites.

Is Mortalive free?
Yes, the core experience is completely free. You can chat as a guest without signing up.

Accounts & Login

Do I need an account?
No. You can start chatting immediately as a guest. Creating an account is optional and allows you to earn a Magnet Score based on positive interactions.

How do I sign up?
Click on "Log in / Sign up" and choose the signup option. Choose a username and password — no email verification is required at this time.

Chat & Matching

How does matching work?
The system finds another user who is also looking to chat. You can add interests to get better matches. If no one is available, you can wait or try again later.

Can I skip a chat?
Yes. You can end the current chat and find a new person at any time by clicking the "Next" button.

Safety & Privacy

Is Mortalive safe?
We take safety seriously. We use snapshot monitoring during video chats, quick report handling, and strict rules against harassment. All chats are anonymous by default.

Can someone find my identity?
No. We do not require personal information. Guests remain completely anonymous.

Technical & Support

Why is my camera not working?
Make sure you have granted camera and microphone permissions in your browser. Try refreshing the page or using a different browser.

How can I contact support?
Use the report button during a chat or email us at support@mortalive.in for any serious issues.
